---
title: "operations"
description: Operators used in logic statements.
category: Control Flow
---

* **==** equal
* **!=** not equal
* **\>** bigger than
* **\<** less than
* **\>=** bigger or equal
* **\<=** less or equal
* **or** this or that
* **and** must be this and that
* **contains** includes the substring if used on a string, or element if used on an array
